About

I'm Clayton Campbell, a student of the code and master of the keyboard. I've started my trek onto the path of a coding monk in 2016 and have truly delved into its depths starting in 2018. I have learned so much, yet as technology advances, so does the effort needed to learn. I'm ready for any challenge that may be thrown my way.
